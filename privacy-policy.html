Information we collect

When you contact WIF Marketing, we may receive your name, email address, phone number, company details, service interest and message content. The website may also collect basic technical information such as browser type, pages visited and referring sources through analytics tools if configured.

How information is used

  • To respond to audit, service and partnership inquiries
  • To understand which pages and offers are useful to visitors
  • To improve website performance, content and conversion paths
  • To maintain basic business records related to inquiries

Sharing and storage

WIF Marketing does not sell personal information. Inquiry information may be shared with internal team members or service providers only when needed to respond, schedule a call or operate the website.

Your choices

You can request correction or deletion of inquiry information by emailing business@wifmarketing.co. Some records may be retained when required for legitimate business, legal or security reasons.
