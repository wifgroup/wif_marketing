Jivan Amrut D2C Launch Case Study

This case shows the early D2C problem: moving from offline presence to online learning without pretending the first campaign is the final growth model.

The Situation

The Ayurveda brand needed an online launch path and first-sale learning. The commercial challenge was building a digital acquisition system that could generate actionable insights from day one.

WIF Focus

The work supported product clarity, MVP movement, and paid acquisition tests — focusing on fast learning loops rather than assumptions.

Key Insight

Early D2C growth depends on fast learning from offer, page, and buyer response. The first campaign is a learning tool, not the final growth model.

Results

First sales achieved — the brand moved from offline-only presence to a functioning digital acquisition pipeline with measurable buyer response data.
