AI Calling Agent Lead Qualification Case Study

This case explains where AI calling fits: after a lead is captured and before sales loses the context needed to follow up well.

The Situation

Campaign leads needed faster first response and clearer qualification. WIF focused on improving call logic, summaries, tags, and routing rules as part of the marketing system.

Key Insight

Automation helps when it gives sales usable context, not just another notification.

Approach

  • Connected paid lead forms to an AI calling workflow
  • AI agent confirms interest, captures context, and routes qualified prospects
  • Structured qualification tags enable better CRM routing

Result

Faster follow-up — reported lead qualification improvement across the funnel.
