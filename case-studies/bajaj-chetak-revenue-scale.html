How Bajaj Chetak Scaled Revenue with WIF Marketing

The Challenge

Bajaj Chetak, a leading electric vehicle manufacturer, needed to generate qualified leads for their EV lineup in the Surat and Gujarat market. They were competing against established players with significantly larger marketing budgets.

Our Approach

We deployed a multi-channel performance marketing strategy combining:

  • Google Ads — Search and display campaigns targeting high-intent EV buyers
  • Meta Ads — Social media campaigns highlighting product features and local availability
  • SEO — Content optimization for EV-related search queries in the Gujarat region

Results

Within 90 days of launching the campaign, Bajaj Chetak saw:

  • 2,400+ qualified leads generated
  • 40% reduction in cost per lead compared to previous campaigns
  • 5.2x return on ad spend

Key Takeaways

  1. Localized targeting significantly outperforms generic campaigns
  2. Combining paid search with social ads creates a multiplier effect
  3. Continuous optimization is essential for maintaining ROI at scale
