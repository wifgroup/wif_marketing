Bajaj Chetak EV Lead Generation Case Study

Bajaj Chetak EV lead generation case study covering paid acquisition, conversion path optimization, and follow-up quality as a unified lead-generation system.

The Situation

EV buyers needed a clear path from initial search or ad interest to showroom action. This required connecting paid acquisition, landing page clarity, and sales follow-up into one measurable system.

WIF Focus

Campaigns, page clarity, and follow-up context were treated as one lead-generation system. WIF aligned acquisition strategy with conversion path design and post-lead qualification workflows.

Approach

  • Paid Acquisition — Targeted Google Ads and Meta campaigns reaching high-intent EV buyers in Surat and Gujarat
  • Landing Page Optimization — Conversion-focused pages with clear CTAs and showroom visit booking paths
  • Follow-Up System — Structured lead handoff with CRM integration and response tracking

Results

Current site data reports 800+ leads at an 18.75% conversion rate. This was achieved through coordinated paid acquisition and conversion-focused follow-up.

Key Takeaway

The best next step is an audit of the same layers in your funnel: source, page, signal, and response. Use this pattern to test against your own funnel before assuming the same result.
