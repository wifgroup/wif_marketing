Workflow Automation CRM Routing Case Study

This case shows why campaign performance is also an operations problem: leads need source context, ownership, and next steps after capture.

The Situation

Ad and website inquiries needed a cleaner path into sales follow-up. Leads were being captured but lost context between marketing and sales teams.

WIF Focus

The workflow attached source context, assigned ownership, and made reminders visible. Marketing and sales alignment was built into the automation model.

Key Insight

Better routing helps teams judge which campaigns produce useful conversations. When every lead carries source context, sales can prioritize and personalize follow-up.

Results

Cleaner handoff — the automation model moves inquiries from ad forms into CRM stages, assigns ownership, sends alerts, and keeps source context attached throughout the pipeline.
