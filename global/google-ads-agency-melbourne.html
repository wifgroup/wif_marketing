What WIF checks first

Where Melbourne Google Ads accounts typically leak budget before the audit.

The same structural problems appear in most Melbourne accounts: keyword targeting that is too broad, conversion tracking that doesn't match CRM reality, and landing pages that break message continuity from the ad. WIF reviews these before recommending budget changes.

Search quality

Actual search terms decide whether Melbourne clicks have buyer intent.

WIF reviews the search terms report, not just keyword lists. Broad match, Performance Max and auto-applied recommendations are the most common sources of wasted spend in Melbourne accounts.

Tracking accuracy

Reported conversions and real sales often show completely different pictures.

WIF validates conversion event setup, checks whether phone calls, form fills and qualified leads are tracked separately, and confirms whether the data reflects what actually reached the CRM.

Page and offer fit

Melbourne buyers decide in the first few seconds whether the page matches what they clicked.

WIF reviews offer specificity, social proof, page load speed and the path between ad message and landing page action before increasing any budget.
