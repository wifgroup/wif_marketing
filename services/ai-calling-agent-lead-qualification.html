Short answer

What an AI calling agent should do for marketing teams.

The agent is not a gimmick. It should answer a practical operations problem: which leads are real, what do they want and who follows up next?

Call script

Questions are mapped to intent, urgency, location and product interest.

The script should collect only the context sales can actually use.

Qualification logic

Responses are tagged by fit and follow-up priority.

Hot, warm and low-fit leads should not enter the same sales queue.

CRM handoff

Summaries and source context stay attached.

Sales teams need campaign, offer and call notes together to respond well.
