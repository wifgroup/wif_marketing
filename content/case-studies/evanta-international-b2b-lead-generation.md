# Evanta International B2B Lead Generation Case Study

This case is about international B2B demand, where category clarity and buyer trust matter before a lead is worth pursuing.

## The Situation

A global B2B audience needed clearer positioning and lead-generation presence. The work had to respect international buyer behavior and longer sales cycles.

## WIF Focus

The work emphasized commercial page clarity, B2B targeting, and international buyer context — ensuring the right message reached decision-makers across regions.

## Key Insight

B2B visibility is stronger when the page explains fit, proof, and the next sales step clearly to an international audience.

## Results

**Global reach and lead-generation momentum** — strengthened international visibility with improved positioning across key B2B channels.
