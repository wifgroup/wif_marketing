# Revolt Motors EV Marketing Case Study

This case looks at EV market entry as a local demand system: visibility, showroom interest, paid acquisition, and follow-up all had to work together.

## The Situation

A new EV market needed launch visibility and demand capture across local buyers. The challenge was building awareness and converting interest into showroom visits in a competitive market.

## WIF Focus

The work connected paid media, local campaign messaging, and showroom follow-up into a coordinated growth execution plan.

## Key Insight

EV market entry succeeds when visibility, interest capture, and response handling work as one system — not as separate campaigns.

## Results

Current site data reports **Rs. 1 Cr+ revenue across six months** — driven by coordinated launch execution across digital channels.
