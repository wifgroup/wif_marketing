Operating playbook

A 30-day rhythm for finding waste, proving signals and scaling carefully.

The audit is not a generic checklist. It turns the current funnel into a practical operating board with owners, channel decisions and measurable next steps.

Week 01

Audit the current machine

Review offer, ad accounts, search demand, landing pages, tracking events, CRM stages, response speed and lead quality.

Week 02

Rebuild the campaign logic

Separate intent groups, define test budgets, fix conversion events, write new hooks and map the page sections each campaign needs.

Week 03

Launch controlled tests

Start with controlled spend, monitor early conversion behavior, check search terms, review sales feedback and pause weak segments fast.

Week 04

Move budget with evidence

Scale the campaigns, pages and audiences showing useful cost, lead quality and pipeline movement, then document the next testing queue.
