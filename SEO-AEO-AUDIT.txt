# SEO and AEO Audit - WIF Marketing

Date: 2026-05-06

This audit is based on the local repo crawl after the updates. Google guidance used: Search Essentials, link best practices, helpful people-first content, structured data guidelines, image SEO, and AI features guidance. Google states that AI Overviews/AI Mode use the same foundational SEO requirements: crawlable/indexable pages, helpful content, textual content, high-quality images, matching structured data, and internal discoverability. Ranking and AI citations are not guaranteed.

## Sitewide Diagnosis

- All 45 HTML pages are now reachable from `index.html` through crawlable `<a href>` links.
- Previously orphaned service, industry and global pages now have sitewide footer links plus contextual links from the homepage and services hub.
- Previously low-visibility proof and resource pages now have footer/hub links and stronger contextual routes.
- Detail pages now have unique answer-first sections and FAQs instead of repeated generic template copy.
- JSON-LD was refreshed with Organization/ProfessionalService, WebPage, Breadcrumb, Service/Article/CreativeWork and FAQ nodes where visible content supports it.
- Generated homepage images were replaced with more realistic photographic assets and descriptive alt text.
